## Design Context

### Users
Local football players and team organizers in Bangladesh, primarily ages 16-35, who want to check slot availability and book the turf quickly. Secondary users are admins managing bookings and blocking slots.

### Brand Personality
Electrifying, gritty, premium. The interface should feel fast, charged, and confident, with the emotional tone of a high-end football campaign rather than a generic booking utility.

### Aesthetic Direction
Stay dark. Keep neon green on black as the core identity and push it toward a premium sports-campaign look inspired by Nike Football campaign sites, Apple TV+ sports drops, and EA FC interface polish. Avoid anything that feels like a generic SaaS booking tool or a bright, friendly sports center site.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with atmosphere: cinematic lighting, dramatic contrast, and motion that feels like stadium energy.
2. Keep booking fast: the landing page should sell the venue while still making availability and booking access immediate.
3. Make every section feel branded: avoid templated card grids and generic marketing layouts.
4. Use neon with discipline: green should feel like an identity signal, not a decorative wash over the entire page.
5. Preserve clarity under intensity: premium visuals should never make navigation, reading, or calls to action feel slower.
